Born and raised in WA, where the rain can make for months with time to fill (with virtual adventures). I graduated from Wesleyan University back in 2015, followed by a brief stint as a member of the Undead Labs QA Team on the canceled project Moonrise. From there, I took a job as a Software Engineer for SRC Inc. starting in the winter of 2015. Out there in Syracuse, NY, my time was spent working on a cross-disciplinary collection of projects - from client-server backends to GUIs. In the end, I was overconfident in my ability to put down roots & thrive in any environment. At the beginning of 2017 I returned to the PNW to rebuild my foundation and pursue a career in the video game industry.
